History of Studio Oneki

Studio oneki is a multidisciplinary art & design studio founded by Aya Shalkar in 2021 in Almaty, Kazakhstan. Studio oneki’s practice is built on experimental and heritage-based research: it looks for new and unseen in the old and forgotten. With its many upcoming projects and collaborations studio oneki is building a new identity of contemporary art and design of Central Asia.




Törtköz körpe

“Törtköz körpe” is a limited series of kazakh traditional patchwork pieces in three colorways: kök köz, sary köz and kúlgin köz. Körpes have always been an important piece of applied arts in Kazakh culture. Just like any other forms of patchwork around the world, the universal tradition of reassembling things to give them new purpose continues throughout centuries. Törtköz körpe is a series of objects that explore cultural design in context of modern use.

Kese Sham

Kese sham is a scented pure soy wax candle in a hand-made traditional ceramic teacup. The “UI” (“HOME”) scent is a smooth blend of freshly baked bread, bergamot tea and a top note of soft powder, that reminds us of our grandmas: the smell that can take you home no matter where you are.
